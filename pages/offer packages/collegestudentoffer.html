College Student Offer

Global Fun City offers exciting discounts on park entry for college students. Students below the age of 22 years can avail a flat 20% discount on park tickets

So, hurry up and plan your college outing with your friends at our water park!

How to book tickets online with a College ID offer?

  • Visit bookings.globalfuncity.com
  • Add tickets from the category - Adult (College ID Offer)
  • 20% discount will be applied automatically
  • Tickets will be sent via email or SMS once the transaction is completed
  • This offer can also be availed at the park by providing your original college photo ID at the ticket counters.
